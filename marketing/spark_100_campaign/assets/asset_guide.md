# Spark 100 Campaign - Creative Assets Guide

This folder contains visual assets designed to support the "Impact & Co-creation" marketing strategy.

## 1. Co-creation Hero (`co_creation_hero.png`)
**Theme:** "Build Together" / Collaboration
**Visual:** Ghanaian father and daughter engaged with a laptop, smiling and pointing.
**Usage:**
- **Primary:** Landing Page Hero Section (Alternative to current hero).
- **Social Media:** LinkedIn posts targeting professional parents.
- **Email:** Header image for the "Invitation to Co-create" email sequence.
**Caption Angle:** "Don't just sign them up. Sit down with them and build their future. Join us as a Founding Family."

## 2. Future Impact Hero (`future_impact_hero.png`)
**Theme:** "Future Ready" / Empowerment
**Visual:** Close-up of a young girl focused on building a robotics/electronics project.
**Usage:**
- **Primary:** Instagram & Facebook Ads.
- **Print:** Brochure cover.
- **WhatsApp:** Status updates to drive urgency.
**Caption Angle:** "She's not just playing. She's engineering her future. Give her the tools she needs with STEAM Spark."

## 3. Founding Family Badge (`founding_family_badge.png`)
**Theme:** Exclusivity / Trust
**Visual:** Premium gold and blue vector seal.
**Usage:**
- **Website:** Trust badge near CTAs or pricing.
- **Email:** Footer signature for "Official Invitation" emails.
- **Social:** Overlay on other images to denote "Spark 100" status.
**Caption Angle:** "Secure your status. Only 100 families will ever hold this badge."
