# Spark 100 Campaign - Creative Assets Guide

This folder contains visual assets designed to support the "Impact & Co-creation" marketing strategy. Each asset is tailored to a specific emotional hook or audience segment.

## 1. Co-creation Hero (`co_creation_hero.png`)
**Theme:** "Build Together" / Collaboration
**Visual:** Ghanaian father and daughter engaged with a laptop, smiling and pointing.
**Usage Context:**
- **Goal:** Show parents that they are active participants, not just customers.
- **Channels:** LinkedIn posts, Email Headers (Welcome Sequence).
**Suggested Copy:**
> "Don't just sign them up. Sit down with them and build their future. Join us as a Founding Family and co-create the perfect learning path."

## 2. Future Impact Hero (`future_impact_hero.png`)
**Theme:** "Future Ready" / Empowerment
**Visual:** Close-up of a young girl focused on building a robotics/electronics project.
**Usage Context:**
- **Goal:** Highlight the tangible skills and confidence children gain.
- **Channels:** Instagram Ads, Facebook Ads (Retargeting).
**Suggested Copy:**
> "She's not just playing. She's engineering her future. Give her the tools she needs with STEAM Spark."

## 3. Family Pride Hero (`family_pride_hero.png`)
**Theme:** "Visible Progress" / Joy
**Visual:** Mother showing child's tablet achievement to a grandparent/friend.
**Usage Context:**
- **Goal:** Appeal to the desire for shared family success and tracking progress.
- **Channels:** WhatsApp Status, Community Group Posts.
**Suggested Copy:**
> "Finally, see exactly what they're learning. Share their wins with the whole family. Track every milestone with our new parent dashboard."

## 4. Curiosity Science Hero (`curiosity_science_hero.png`)
**Theme:** "Igniting Spark" / Wonder
**Visual:** Young boy looking with wonder at a science experiment.
**Usage Context:**
- **Goal:** Capture the magic of learning and the "spark" moment.
- **Channels:** Top-of-Funnel Ads (Awareness), Print Brochures.
**Suggested Copy:**
> "That look of wonder? That's the Spark. We help you keep it alive every single day."

## 5. Founding Family Badge (`founding_family_badge.png`)
**Theme:** Exclusivity / Trust
**Visual:** Premium gold and blue vector seal.
**Usage Context:**
- **Goal:** Create FOMO (Fear Of Missing Out) and establish prestige.
- **Placement:** Near CTA buttons, Email Footers, Overlay on social posts.
**Suggested Copy:**
> "Secure your status. Only 100 families will ever hold this badge."

## 6. Official Logo (`steam_spark_logo.png`)
**Theme:** Brand Identity
**Visual:** The official STEAM Spark logo.
**Usage Context:**
- **Placement:** Always include in corner of ad creatives or as profile picture for campaign accounts.
- **Context:** Ensures brand recognition across all channels.
