## üí° Pro Tips for Success

### Messaging:
- **Lead with emotion, close with logic** ‚Äî Start with child's future, end with pricing/features
- **Use parent names and child names** ‚Äî Personalization increases conversion by 40%
- **Show, don't tell** ‚Äî Screenshots > descriptions

### Timing:
- **Best posting times (Ghana):** 7-9 AM, 12-2 PM, 7-9 PM
- **Email send times:** Tuesday/Thursday 8 AM (highest open rates)
- **WhatsApp optimal:** Evenings after work (6-8 PM)

### Response Protocol:
- **Reply to DMs within 1 hour** during business hours
- **Use voice notes on WhatsApp** for warmth (vs. text)
- **Offer to call** if parent seems hesitant

### Conversion Tactics:
- **Scarcity:** Always mention spots remaining
- **Social proof:** Share recent signups ("Just helped Mrs. Mensah enroll Ama!")
- **Remove friction:** Offer to walk them through signup on a call
