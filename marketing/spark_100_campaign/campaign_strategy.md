# The Spark 100 Launch Campaign
## Strategic Marketing Plan

### Campaign Overview
**Objective:** Secure the first 100 platform bookings within 30 days, transitioning from manual service to tech-enabled marketplace.

**Target Audience:**
- **Primary:** Existing 150 families (warm leads)
- **Secondary:** New parents in Accra's middle-upper class neighborhoods
- **Tertiary:** International schools & educational institutions

---

## Campaign Pillars

### 1. Impact & Co-creation
**Psychology:** Parents care most about their child's future and being heard.
- "Build the Perfect Platform for Your Child"
- "Shape the Future of STEAM Education"
- "Founding Family Status"

### 2. Priority Access
**Psychology:** Fear of missing out on the best resources.
- "Skip the Line for Top Tutors"
- "First Access to New Features"
- "Exclusive Community"

### 3. Tangible Value (Secondary)
**Psychology:** A "no-brainer" perk to reduce risk.
- "1 Year Zero Platform Fees (Save GHS 480)"
- "Concierge Onboarding"

---

## Campaign Timeline (30 Days)

### Week 1: Internal Launch (Days 1-7)
**Focus:** Existing families + top teachers

**Actions:**
- Direct WhatsApp messages to all 150 families
- Email announcement with exclusive early access link
- Teacher onboarding sessions (help create profiles)
- Social media teaser campaign

**Goal:** 30 bookings

### Week 2: School Partnerships (Days 8-14)
**Focus:** B2B institutional deals

**Actions:**
- Send proposals to 10 international schools in Accra
- Offer "School Demo Day" (free robotics workshop)
- Parent association presentations
- LinkedIn outreach to school administrators

**Goal:** 25 bookings (from school referrals)

### Week 3: Social Expansion (Days 15-21)
**Focus:** Organic reach via word-of-mouth

**Actions:**
- Referral incentive launch (both referrer & referee get GHS 50 credit)
- Instagram/Facebook ad campaign targeting Accra parents
- TikTok influencer partnerships (education-focused creators)
- WhatsApp Status campaign (teachers share their profiles)

**Goal:** 30 bookings

### Week 4: Final Push (Days 22-30)
**Focus:** Converting fence-sitters

**Actions:**
- "Last 15 Spots" urgency messaging
- Flash sales (Friday-Sunday only)
- Success story carousel (showcase early adopters)
- Community event: "STEAM Fair" for families

**Goal:** 15 bookings

## Channel Strategy

### 1. WhatsApp (Primary for Ghana)
- **Personal messages** from Triumph/team to existing network
- **Broadcast lists** for announcements
- **Status updates** with countdown graphics
- **Group invites** for Q&A sessions

### 2. Email Marketing
- **Sequence 1:** Announcement → Features → Testimonial → Last Chance
- **Sequence 2:** (Teachers) Onboarding → Profile Tips → First Booking Celebration
- **Segment:** Active vs. Dormant families

### 3. Social Media (Instagram + Facebook)
- **Organic:** Daily posts showcasing platform features
- **Paid:** Retargeting ads to website visitors
- **Influencers:** Partner with 3 parenting/education influencers in Accra
- **User-Generated Content:** Encourage families to share child's roadmap screenshots

### 4. Direct Sales (School Partnerships)
- **Proposal Deck:** Professional PDF with case studies
- **Demo Video:** 2-minute walkthrough of teacher + parent dashboards
- **Relationship Building:** Coffee meetings with decision-makers

---

## Conversion Funnel

### Awareness → Interest
**Touchpoint:** Social media ad, WhatsApp status, school event
**Hook:** "Ghana's First STEAM Education Marketplace"

### Interest → Consideration
**Touchpoint:** Landing page visit
**Content:** Video testimonial, platform tour, pricing transparency

### Consideration → Decision
**Touchpoint:** Email sequence, retargeting ad
**Nudge:** Scarcity ("73 spots left"), social proof (live booking feed)

### Decision → Action
**Touchpoint:** Booking page
**Friction Removal:** One-click signup via Google, mobile money payment, instant tutor match

---

## Success Metrics

### Primary KPIs
- **Bookings:** 100 (target) / 120 (stretch)
- **Conversion Rate:** 15%+ (families contacted → bookings)
- **CAC:** < GHS 50 per booking

### Secondary KPIs
- **Teacher Activation:** 30 teachers with complete profiles
- **School Partnerships:** 2 signed MOUs
- **Social Engagement:** 5,000+ impressions, 500+ clicks

### Vanity Metrics (for PR/Investors)
- **Waitlist Size:** Aim for 200+ after 100 slots fill
- **Media Mentions:** Local tech blogs, education forums
- **NPS Score:** Survey first 50 families

---

## Risk Mitigation

### Risk 1: Slow Teacher Onboarding
**Mitigation:** Concierge onboarding (we fill profiles for them), incentive (GHS 100 bonus for first completed profile)

### Risk 2: Payment Friction
**Mitigation:** Support hotline via WhatsApp, video tutorials for mobile money, manual fallback

### Risk 3: Low Parent Tech Adoption
**Mitigation:** In-person onboarding sessions, simplified UI, "Kids Mode" for roadmaps
