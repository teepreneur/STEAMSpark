## Email Design Guidelines

### Header:
- STEAM Spark logo (left-aligned)
- Navigation: About | Educators | How It Works (subtle gray text)

### Hero Section:
- Bold headline (36pt, purple)
- Supporting text (18pt, dark gray)
- CTA button (bright orange, white text, rounded corners)

### Body:
- Max width: 600px
- Font: Inter or similar sans-serif
- Line height: 1.6
- Bullet points with checkmark icons (not standard bullets)

### Footer:
- Social media icons
- Unsubscribe link
- Contact info: hello@steamsparkgh.com
