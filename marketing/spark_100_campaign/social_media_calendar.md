## Content Creation Guidelines

### Visuals:
- **Brand colors:** Purple (#7c3aed) + Orange/Spark accent
- **Font:** Clean, modern sans-serif
- **Image style:** Bright, high-energy, diverse children
- **Logo placement:** Bottom right corner on all graphics

### Tone of Voice:
- **Inspirational** (not salesy)
- **Educational** (teach while you promote)
- **Authentic** (real stories, real families)
- **Optimistic** (future-focused language)

### Call-to-Action Hierarchy:
1. "Join the Spark 100" (primary)
2. "Link in bio"
3. "DM us for details"
4. "Tag a friend who needs this"
